Archival review: 

This was an extremely interesting course. It largely involved terminology and felt as if really was a course designed to bring us up to speed on the language of computers and mathematics. Relations seemed to be the most difficult while Algorythms and Trees were the easiest.

I recieved a B in the course, but I felt it was deserved. The test focusing on permutations brought me down, but I think some review of the material could be good. After emailing Gao about futher reading, she recommended the chapters on finite state machine and Turing machines.
